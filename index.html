About Me

My name is Virginia Walkup. I am from Rock Hill, SC and have a brother and a sister. We are triplets, all a minute apart. I love animals and being outside. Hiking and playing sports are some of my favorite things to do. I have a dog named Daisy and she is a labradoodle. I go to the University of South Carolina and am a biology major. I love science and am very curious about how things work, I've always been that way.

A few of my hobbies include reading, cooking, and spending time outside. I love to read classic fiction books. My favorite is the Hound of the Baskervilles by Sir Arthur Connan Doyle. I also love cooking. I watch a lot of the food network and am always looking out for a new recipe to try. I don't have a sweet tooth so I am more about the savory types of foods. Lastly, spending time outside is something that I have always loved to do. I love walking, running, and playing sports. I used to play lots of soccer but now have moved on to tennis. These things bring me lots of joy in my life.
